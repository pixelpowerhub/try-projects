The Short Version

We don't sell your data. We don't build profiles on you. Most of our tools work right there in your browser and never send your content to us at all. That's the summary — read on for the full details.

Ownership of Content and Right to Publish

Everything on PixelPowerHub.in — the tools, the writing, the designs, the graphics, all of it — belongs to Pixel Power Hub, unless we've said otherwise somewhere specific.

We keep the right to create, publish, edit, update, remove, or otherwise manage our own content on this site, whenever we need to.

We exercise that right under Article 19(1)(a) of the Constitution of India, which protects freedom of speech and expression. We use it responsibly, to publish educational and community-focused content about technology, finance, accessibility, and digital learning.

Please don't copy, reuse, redistribute, or republish our content without asking us first — unless we've explicitly said it's okay somewhere.

Your Data and Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data, and we don't use it for anything beyond running and improving our own services.

Whenever our tools process your data, it's handled temporarily and, wherever we can manage it, directly on your own device. We don't build profiles on our users, and we don't use personal data for advertising or tracking beyond what the law or our platform providers require.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, we'll update this page and, where it makes sense, post a notice on the site. If you keep using Pixel Power Hub after a change goes live, that counts as accepting the updated policy.
