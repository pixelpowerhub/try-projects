    Why files sometimes disappear on large transfers
    • Phones can reclaim memory from a background browser tab, especially one holding a large file — this can force the whole page to reload and clear your selection.
    • Use the Share button to send your key through WhatsApp or any app — it opens a share sheet without fully leaving Pixel Share, which is safer than switching apps manually.
    • Once transferring, try to keep this tab open and in view rather than minimising it for long periods.
    • If your selection does get cleared, come back here and choose the files again — your name and place in the app are remembered.

    Good to know
    • Files are held in your browser's memory as they arrive, then handed to you as normal downloads — nothing touches a server.
    • Large transfers use more memory; close other heavy tabs if a big transfer feels slow.
    • If the sender doesn't respond to your request, ask them to check their screen — the approval prompt is easy to miss.
    • If the connection drops, ask the sender for the key again and try once more.
