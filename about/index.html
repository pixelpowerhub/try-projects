PixelPowerHub is a platform focused on making digital knowledge simple, accessible, and useful — especially for the visually impaired community.

We cover technology, stock market basics, digital tools, and also run live gaming streams to create an open and engaging learning environment.

We started on YouTube by sharing clear and practical content. Over time, many people asked us the same question — “Which product or tool is actually accessible and right for me?” That led us to create PixelPowerHub.in as a central page for trusted information and learning resources.

We also offer structured online courses on computers, Android, stock market fundamentals, and other digital skills to help people learn in a practical and confident way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make digital knowledge — including technology, finance, and online tools — accessible, understandable, and usable for everyone, regardless of visual ability.

Our Principles

Clarity Simple and practical explanations
Honesty Unbiased and reliable information
Accessibility Content designed for everyone
Usefulness Focused on real-world skills

PixelPowerHub was founded on 27 June 2024.

We also run a separate sports channel called Sports Club Official India, focused on sports and community engagement.
