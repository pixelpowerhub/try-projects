Pixel Power Hub is a place to make digital knowledge simple and useful for everyone — with a special focus on the visually impaired community.

We cover technology, the basics of the stock market, and everyday digital tools, and we run live gaming streams too. The idea is simple: learning should feel open and welcoming, not intimidating.

We started out on YouTube, sharing clear, no-nonsense content. Over time, the same question kept coming up from viewers: “Which tool or product is actually accessible, and right for me?” That question is why PixelPowerHub.in exists — one place you can come back to for trustworthy information and learning resources.

We've also built structured online courses covering computers, Android, stock market basics, and other digital skills, so you can learn at your own pace and actually feel confident using what you've learned.

Our Mission

We want digital knowledge — technology, finance, online tools, all of it — to be something everyone can access, understand, and actually use. Your level of vision shouldn't decide what you're able to learn.

What We Stand For

Clarity We explain things simply, without the jargon.
Honesty Our information is unbiased and something you can rely on.
Accessibility Everything we build is designed with everyone in mind.
Usefulness We focus on skills you'll actually use in real life.

Pixel Power Hub was founded on 27 June 2024.

We also run a separate sports channel called Sports Club Official India, built around sports and community.
