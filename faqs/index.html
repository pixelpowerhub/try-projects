About Pixel Power Hub & Our Vision

What is Pixel Power Hub?

Pixel Power Hub is an independent technology and learning platform offering accessible digital tools, educational resources, and practical guidance. We're focused on making technology simple, privacy-respecting, and usable for everyone — especially visually impaired users.

Who is Pixel Power Hub for?
  • Beginners learning technology
  • Blind and visually impaired users
  • Students and educators
  • Content creators
  • Anyone who cares about privacy

You don't need any advanced technical knowledge to use the platform.

When was Pixel Power Hub founded?

Pixel Power Hub was founded on 27 June 2024.

What's the long-term vision?

We want to simplify technology, protect digital privacy, improve accessibility, give people practical knowledge they can actually use, and build long-term trust through transparency.

Tools & Usage

What kind of tools does Pixel Power Hub offer?

We offer privacy tools, file utilities, accessibility-friendly resources, technology guides, and practical digital skill content — things like Math Master, Password Generator, Text Analyzer, File Locker, Age Calculator, Audio Trimmer, and Document Saver.

Do I need to create an account?

In most cases, no. We avoid forced sign-ups to keep the platform free and easy to use. Math Master is the one exception — it offers an optional saved profile so it can greet you by name, but you can also use it as a guest without saving anything.

Do the tools work offline or need an internet connection?

Most of our tools run entirely in your browser once the page has loaded, so they keep working even with a patchy connection. You'll still need internet the first time to load the page itself.

Can I use the tools on my phone?

Yes — every tool is designed to work on mobile browsers, including with screen readers like TalkBack and VoiceOver. If something feels broken on your device, please tell us using the Contact Us page.

Privacy, Data & Security

Does Pixel Power Hub collect personal data?

We follow a minimal-data policy: we don't sell your data, we don't track you for ads, and we don't ask for information we don't actually need.

What happens to files I upload or work with?

Your files are only used for the specific task you're doing. We don't share them with third parties, and we don't store them permanently unless a tool clearly says otherwise.

Where can I read the full Privacy Policy?

You'll find the complete details on our Privacy Policy page, linked in the footer of every page.

Accessibility

Is Pixel Power Hub accessible for blind and low-vision users?

Yes — accessibility is our foundation, not an afterthought. We use screen-reader-friendly layouts, logical navigation, keyboard support, and skip links throughout the site.

Does the site support dark mode?

Yes. You'll find a dark/light mode toggle in the header on every page, and your choice is remembered the next time you visit.

What if I run into an accessibility problem?

Please tell us — we treat accessibility reports as a priority. You can reach us through the Contact Us page, or read more on our Accessibility Statement.

How Pixel Power Hub Is Supported

Does Pixel Power Hub show ads?

Yes, a limited number of ads help fund our development. They don't influence our content, and they don't compromise your privacy.

Is Pixel Power Hub free to use?

Yes, our tools and articles are free. Some of our courses may involve a cost, which is always shown clearly before you pay for anything.

Independence & Trust

Is Pixel Power Hub affiliated with any big tech company?

No. We operate independently, which lets us stay unbiased and put our users first instead of corporate interests.

Didn't find your answer here?

No problem — reach out through our Contact Us page and we'll get back to you.
