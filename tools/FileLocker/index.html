File Encryptor and Decryptor

🔒 Your Privacy: No password, file, or folder is ever saved on our website backend. Encryption happens entirely in your browser.

  • Pick Encrypt (Lock) to lock files, or Decrypt (Unlock) to open one you've locked before.
  • Select one or more files, set a password (plus optional hint questions), and download your locked file.
  • Keep your password safe — if you lose it and haven't set hints, we can't recover your files.

Need the full step-by-step guide? See the User Guide.
